Château Gustav
Château Gustav is a private wine tasting and consulting practice founded by WSET-trained wine connoisseur Dele Faulkner. Based in Dallas, we serve clients across Dallas, Austin, and Houston.
This app pairs your dish with the right wine using the same flavor chemistry principles taught in WSET, not generic lookup tables.
